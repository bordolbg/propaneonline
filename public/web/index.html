What is Propane Online?

Propane Online is a website where people around the world can sell digital propane for fun. No currency in the game has a valid exchange rate for real money. It's all just fun and games. Look at our leaderboard to see where you stand against other players.

Propane Online is not a real live propane company. It is simply a virtual world for people to sell fake propane online without the use of any real money.
